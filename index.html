The Undercity

In this level, players find themselves in a dazzling utopian punk city—a metropolis where neon and greenery intertwine in perfect harmony. Towering skyscrapers coated in chrome and vibrant graffiti reach skyward, their facades adorned with massive holographic displays and bioluminescent gardens. Clean energy pulses through every building, casting the streets in a soft, rainbow glow. Along the pedestrian walkways, citizens blend avant-garde tech with organic materials, donning wearable devices that look like art pieces as they zip past on hoverboards or electric bikes. Despite the city's polished and futuristic appearance, the underbelly tells a different story, hinting at underlying social tensions. A network of alleys and rooftop hideouts reveals the presence of rebel groups questioning the price of utopia. As players navigate these contrasting layers, they must decide whether to uphold the city fragile order or join forces with the hidden resistance seeking freedom from a sanitized society.
